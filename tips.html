Pre-Workout

Pre-workout is a substance that gives you an energy boost to help you perform better in the gym. Though formulae vary, most use a combination of caffeine, creatine, BCAAs, and Beta-Alanine to do this. Caffeine is a stimulant that can help you perform better mentally and physically. It is recommended to take pre-workout after the first month of gym.

Protein

A popular dietary supplement is protein powder. Protein is a macronutrient that aids in the development of muscle, tissue repair, and the production of enzymes and hormones. Protein powder can help people lose weight and tone their muscles as well. It is recommended to take protein when you start at the gym, but if you don't want to buy it all months, you can get in clean bulk because it is very helpful for your muscles.

Accessories

Gym belt, wristbands, flat shoes, bag, shaker.

Creatine

Creatine is a natural chemical that the body converts to creatine phosphate it gives you. Creatine aids in the constant supply of energy to active muscles by increasing production. It is recommended to use creatine before the Sept month. Do not take it after the Sept month.
